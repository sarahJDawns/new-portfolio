## Core Functionality

* **Authentication & User Profiles**
    * **Secure Access:** Robust sign-up and login flow requiring username, email, and password.
    * **Account Management:** Authenticated users have full control over their presence, including the ability to permanently delete their account and data.

* **Content Creation**
    * **Flexible Posting:** Users can share updates via text-based headings, detailed captions, or high-quality photo uploads.
    * **Content Ownership:** Authors maintain full control with the ability to delete their own posts at any time.

* **Community Engagement**
    * **Interactive Feedback:** Logged-in users can engage with the community by liking posts and individual comments.
    * **Dynamic Commenting:** Full support for nested discussions, allowing users to contribute to and manage their own conversations.
